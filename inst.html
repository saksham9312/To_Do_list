Winnie - To-Do App!

Instructions
1)To do a list click on add list button.
2)To add new task click on "New Task Name".
3) To cut a completed task simply click on the task you want to cut.
4)To clear completed tasks, just click on "Clear Completed Task" button.
5) To delete a list simply click on "Delete list" button
